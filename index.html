About the Hospital

A quieter way to heal, a stricter way to operate.

Durai Gastro Care Hospital was founded with one belief — that gastrointestinal medicine deserves the same craft as the most exacting surgery. From a first endoscopy to a complex pancreatic resection, every step is handled by senior specialists, supported by a calm, modern facility, and held to the standard set by 20+ years at the operating table.

  • Senior surgeon-led care for every admission, no rotating residents.
  • Advanced laparoscopic theatres with HD imaging and modern energy platforms.
  • Multi-disciplinary tumour board for hepatobiliary & GI oncology cases.
  • Transparent pricing & cashless support — no surprises after discharge.
